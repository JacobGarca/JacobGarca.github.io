Light Bot Reflection

The light bot is a lot like a computer. The light bot and a computer both utilize states, which can be manipulated with inputs to give you different outputs and change the state . They will not do anything you don't tell them to do. The light bot is different from a computer in a lot of ways however. While they are both state machines, a computer is a lot more complex . Light bot could utilize proc 1/2, while a computer utilizes thousands of procs, all working together in infinite combinations. Another difference is that light bot was limited to five actions, while a computer has virtually no limits.
